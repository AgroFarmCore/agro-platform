# Glossary

Shared vocabulary keeps module names and product conversations consistent.

## Core Terms

- `web`: public marketing and content surface
- `portal`: authenticated experience for farmers and suppliers
- `backoffice`: internal operations and support interface
- `worker`: background runtime for queues and automations
- `module`: bounded business area inside `packages/modules`
- `contract`: public API, DTO, or validation boundary exposed by a package
- `owner`: team responsible for review and long-term maintenance
- `staging`: integrated pre-production environment
- `smoke check`: short validation after deploy or release candidate

## Product Terms

- `farmer`: customer persona operating production workflows
- `supplier`: customer persona providing catalog or fulfillment inputs
- `recommendation`: generated suggestion or ranking logic for a customer context
- `order`: transactional flow between demand and supply actors
- `catalog`: managed list of products, offers, or supply entries
